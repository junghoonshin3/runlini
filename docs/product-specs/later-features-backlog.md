# Later Features Backlog

These items can make Runlini broader and richer, but they should wait until the
core running, ghost, and analysis experience is reliable.

## Safety and Sharing

- Real-time location sharing
- Safety contacts and live-run beacon flow
- Shareable live progress links

## Coaching and Training

- Personalized training plans
- Guided runs
- Goal-specific plan templates for 5K, 10K, half marathon, and marathon
- Recovery, mobility, and strength add-ons
- Coaching dashboard or coach-managed plans

## Social and Motivation

- Challenges
- Badges and milestone rewards
- Clubs, teams, and friend feeds
- Comments, cheers, and social sharing

## Discovery and Routing

- Route recommendations
- Popular local routes
- Saved route library
- Start-point or terrain-based route search

## Advanced Performance Insights

- VO2 max estimates
- Training load
- Fatigue and readiness trends
- Heat and altitude adaptation insights
- Long-term trend comparisons

## Notes

- Any later feature that adds field risk should stay behind reliability work.
- Features in this file should not slow down the near-term ghost-running core.
